# Question Checklist

Run through this before asking for help:

- Have I checked the official docs, FAQ, or existing examples?
- Have I searched for the error message or symptom directly?
- Can I say what I expected to happen and what actually happened?
- Can I list the exact steps that trigger the problem?
- Did I include my environment, version, and key dependencies?
- Did I say what I already tried and what the result was?
- Did I remove vague phrases like "it does not work" or "help me"?
- Is my title specific enough that someone scanning a forum can understand it fast?

If the answer to several of these is "no", add that information first.
